About

No one likes portable storage buildings that clash with a home or business building. With LP® SmartSide® painted panels, your portable storage buildings can be coordinated to the color scheme of your choice. These painted panels have the quality of engineered wood, but the aesthetic of cedar wood. Not only do these painted panels add character and style to a storage building, they add durability. The panels resist fungal, decay and termites. The manufacturer even provides a 50-year prorated limited material warranty and a 5-year 100% labor and replacement warranty.
